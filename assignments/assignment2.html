Overview

This study benchmarks four distinct neural architectures for text classification using a filtered subset of the News Category Dataset. We evaluate traditional sequence models against modern Transformer-based approaches to identify the best trade-off between performance and training efficiency.

Comparative Results

Experimental results indicate that pre-trained Transformer models significantly outperform custom-trained sequence models. However, the custom RCNN model shows strong performance while being significantly faster to train than fine-tuning a full Transformer.
